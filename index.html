Dr Abdulla

I am an Assistant Professor at Jamia Millia Islamia a Central University , New Delhi, India. Prior to join JMI, I have worked as a Research Associate at the ICAR-National Institute of Agricultural Economics and Policy Research, New Delhi, India.

I have also worked as a Research Associate at National Academy of Agricultural Sciences (NAAS), New Delhi, India and Young Professional at Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India , New Delhi, India

I hold a M. Phil and Ph.D. degree in Economics from the Aligarh Muslim University , Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. The title of my Ph.D. dissertation was “Performance of Sugar Mills in India: A Comparative Study of Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh” and M.Phil dissertation "Efficiency of Sugar Mills In Uttar Pradesh: An Analysis", I earned an M.A. and B. A. (Horns) in Economics along with Mathematics and Statistics from the Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh (U.P.).

My research interests include Poverty and inequality, Trade, Banking, climate change, Climate variability, Climate resilience, Development economics, Environmental economics, and agricultural economics, agricultural marketing & channels, value chain analysis, Agriculture & allied activities, Efficiency & productivity, Sustainable agrifood system (SAS), organic farming, sustainable agriculture system, Sustainable water management and agricultural policy evaluation.
